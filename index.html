Quick intake so we can configure your Claude account, preferences, and projects

This is a short questionnaire to help set up your Claude account so it actually saves you time from day one. Fill in whatever you can, skip what you cannot, and do not overthink it. The more concrete and specific your answers, the better the setup. Plan on 15 to 20 minutes.
